About AUI Lost and Found

The AUI Lost and Found service is managed by Al Akhawayn University to help students, staff, and visitors reclaim lost property. This service provides an online platform to report found items and search for lost belongings conveniently.

Our Mission

To streamline the process of reporting and retrieving lost items, ensuring they are returned to their rightful owners efficiently and with ease.

How It Works

Items found on campus can be posted on our website by anyone. If you believe one of the listed items belongs to you, you can submit a claim form available on our website. All claims are subject to verification to ensure items are returned to their legitimate owners.
